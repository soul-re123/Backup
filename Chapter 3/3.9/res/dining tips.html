Be kind to your server

ALWAYS be kind to your server its common sense. Being rude to your server can make them not want to be attentive to your table and make their and your experience worse.

Pace yourself

Always pace yourself when eating at a restaurant, it may be good but it'll make you seem like you don't care about how other people feel.

Eat when everyone gets their food

It's always nice to eat when everyone has their food so you aren't looking at someone else eat, or their the ones looking at you eat.
